DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) Attack

About

A Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt the normal functioning of a targeted server, service, or network by overwhelming it with a flood of internet traffic from multiple sources.

Impact

DDoS attacks can lead to service disruptions, causing websites or online services to become temporarily or permanently unavailable to users.

Mitigation

Organizations defend against DDoS attacks by using traffic filtering, load balancing, and content delivery networks (CDNs). Early detection and mitigation are crucial for minimizing the impact of DDoS attacks.
