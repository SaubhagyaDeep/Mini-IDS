Web Brute Force Attack

About

A Web Brute Force Attack is a type of cyberattack where an attacker systematically attempts to guess a user's credentials by trying different combinations of usernames and passwords until the correct ones are found.

Impact

Successful brute force attacks can lead to unauthorized access to web applications, compromising user accounts and potentially exposing sensitive information.

Mitigation

To defend against Web Brute Force Attacks, it is essential to enforce strong password policies, implement account lockout mechanisms, and consider the use of multi-factor authentication (MFA) to enhance security.
