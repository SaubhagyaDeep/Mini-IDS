DoS GoldenEye Attack

About

The GoldenEye variant of a Denial of Service (DoS) attack is a type of cyber threat that aims to disrupt the normal functioning of a targeted server or network by overwhelming it with a flood of malicious traffic.

Impact

GoldenEye attacks can lead to service disruptions, making the targeted website or online service temporarily or permanently unavailable to users.

Mitigation

Organizations defend against GoldenEye attacks by implementing network security measures, traffic filtering, and having robust incident response plans.
