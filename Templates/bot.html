Bot Attack

About

A bot attack involves the use of automated scripts, commonly known as bots, to perform various malicious activities on computer systems, networks, or online services.

Impact

Bots can be used for activities such as data scraping, account takeover, distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks, and spreading malware.

Mitigation

Organizations can defend against bot attacks by implementing security measures such as web application firewalls, rate limiting, and regular monitoring of network traffic for suspicious patterns.
