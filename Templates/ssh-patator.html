SSH-patator Attack

About

SSH-patator is a tool used for performing brute-force attacks against SSH servers. It systematically tries different combinations of usernames and passwords until it finds the correct credentials, allowing unauthorized access to the target system.

Impact

Successful SSH-patator attacks can lead to unauthorized access, data theft, and compromise the security of the target system.

Mitigation

To defend against SSH-patator attacks, it is crucial to use strong and complex passwords, implement account lockout policies, and consider the use of multi-factor authentication (MFA) for an additional layer of security.
