Port Scan Attack

About

Port scanning is a technique used by attackers to identify open ports on a target system. This information can be exploited to launch further attacks, gain unauthorized access, or gather intelligence about the target's network architecture.

Impact

Port scanning alone does not directly cause harm, but it is often a precursor to more advanced attacks. It helps attackers identify potential vulnerabilities in a system.

Mitigation

Organizations can defend against port scanning by implementing firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regularly monitoring and logging network activities.
